Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 11, 2026

At JSA & Co., the privacy and confidentiality of our clients' information are of paramount importance. This Privacy Policy outlines our practices regarding the collection, use, and protection of your personal and legal data.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect personal information such as your name, contact details, financial records, and case-specific documentation necessary to provide comprehensive legal representation.

2. Use of Information

Your information is used strictly for the purpose of legal consultation, representation, and compliance with statutory obligations. We do not sell or share your data with unauthorized third parties.

3. Attorney-Client Privilege

All communications and documents shared with our attorneys are bound by strict attorney-client privilege and confidentiality agreements, ensuring your maximum protection under the law.
