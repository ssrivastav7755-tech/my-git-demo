Our Journey

Three Decades of Excellence

A proud legacy built on landmark achievements, unwavering ethics, and legal innovation.

1991

Firm Founded

JSA Advocates & Solicitors established in New Delhi by Jyoti Sagar, with a vision to provide best-in-class legal services.

1998

Mumbai Expansion

Opened our Mumbai office to strengthen capabilities in banking, finance, and capital markets advisory.

2005

First Chambers Ranking

Ranked by Chambers & Partners for the first time — the beginning of a tradition of global recognition.

2012

Pan-India Presence

Expanded to 6 offices across New Delhi, Mumbai, Bengaluru, Hyderabad, Chennai, and Kolkata.

2018

IFLR1000 Top Tier

Consistently ranked as a Top Tier Firm by IFLR1000, with 12 practice areas and 50+ ranked lawyers.

2024

Excellence Continues

Advising on some of India's most complex cross-border transactions, regulatory matters, and landmark litigation.
